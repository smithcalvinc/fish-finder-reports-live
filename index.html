Fishing search questions

How far does a town search look?

Approximately 50 miles. The actual results depend on available official water names, map coverage and the ability to identify the town correctly.

Does an empty result mean there is no fishing nearby?

No. It means the beta search did not return a reliable local match. Use the linked official state fishing directory or report the missing water.

Are all listed waters guaranteed public?

No. Known private and closed waters are filtered, but shoreline, launch and seasonal access can change. Always verify current agency information and posted signs.
